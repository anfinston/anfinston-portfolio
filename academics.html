PET & MRI Image Fusion using Transformer Residual U-Net Model

Research work focusing on improving the classification of Alzheimer's Disease by combining PET and MRI images through a Transformer Residual U-Net based fusion pipeline, followed by an adaptive ensemble classifier for robust decision-making.
