About Me

I am F. D. Anfinston, Head of the Department – Electronics and Communication Engineering at Achariya College of Engineering Technology, Puducherry. I enjoy building disciplined, value-based learning environments and mentoring students to become confident, industry-ready engineers.

With 13+ years of academic experience, I work at the intersection of Electronics, Communication and AI-driven medical imaging. My current research focuses on PET/MRI image fusion and deep-learning based analysis for healthcare applications.

Academic Profile

  • HoD – Electronics & Communication Engineering, Achariya CET.
  • IQAC Coordinator, driving NAAC, quality initiatives and documentation.
  • Controller of Examinations / Exam Cell Lead for the institution.
  • University Nodal Officer for academic communication & compliance.
  • Completed AICTE Sponsored QIP PG Course in AI & Data Science at IIIT Kottayam – eligible to handle AI & DS subjects as per AICTE norms.

Teaching & Mentoring Philosophy

I strongly believe that engineering education must blend concept clarity, practical exposure and character formation. My classrooms focus on:

  • Clear fundamentals in core ECE subjects through real-time examples.
  • Hands-on experiments, mini-projects and coding-based learning.
  • Encouraging students to ask questions, present, and teach peers.
  • Connecting technology with social responsibility and values.

Research Interests

  • Medical image fusion using PET/MRI and transformer-based architectures.
  • Deep learning for healthcare diagnostics and decision support.
  • Signal & image processing applications in biomedical systems.
  • AI & Data Science for education and academic analytics.
