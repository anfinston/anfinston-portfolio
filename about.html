Hello, I’m F. D. Anfinston

HoD – Electronics and Communication Engineering, IQAC Coordinator and academic leader at Achariya College of Engineering Technology, Puducherry.

Short Professional Bio

I am an academician with over 13 years of experience in teaching, mentoring and administration in Electronics and Communication Engineering. At present, I head the ECE department at Achariya College of Engineering Technology (ACET), where I focus on building strong fundamentals, discipline and employable skills among students.

I lead institutional quality initiatives as the IQAC Coordinator of Achariya CET and have been involved in NAAC, NBA and autonomous-status related documentation, audits and quality systems.

My research interests lie in AI-powered medical imaging, especially PET/MRI image fusion and Alzheimer’s disease classification using transformer-based residual U-Net models. I actively guide student projects and mini-projects in image processing, IoT and AI-based applications.
