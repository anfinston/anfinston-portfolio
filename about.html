About Me

Academician, mentor and department head in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Professional Summary

I am an engineering educator with more than a decade of experience in teaching, academic planning and department-level leadership. Over the years, I have worked closely with students, faculty, university bodies and management to build a disciplined and caring learning environment.

Currently, I serve as Head of the Department in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Achariya College of Engineering Technology, Puducherry. My work integrates classroom teaching, academic administration, examination coordination and quality assurance processes like IQAC and NAAC.

To know more about my responsibilities, you can visit the Experience page or see my current research focus on the Academics page.

Current Focus

  • Encouraging project-based and skill-based learning
  • Supporting students for placements and higher studies
  • Exploring AI/ML in medical imaging as part of my Ph.D.
